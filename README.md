# AI-Based Carbon Emission Prediction and Green Logistics Optimization System (LogiBots)

## Overview
LogiBots is an AI-driven system designed to optimize logistics operations by predicting **CO₂ emissions and vehicle maintenance needs**. The solution supports sustainable logistics and fuel-efficient operations aligned with Oman Vision 2040.

This project was developed and presented at the **MCBS Hackathon**, where it secured **3rd place**.

---

## Problem Statement
Logistics companies often prioritize cost over environmental impact, leading to increased carbon emissions and inefficient operations. There is a need for data-driven systems to monitor emissions and optimize logistics sustainably.

---

## Solution Approach

### 1. CO₂ Emission Prediction (Regression)
- Built a **Random Forest Regressor** to predict CO₂ emissions  
- Evaluated using:
  - Mean Absolute Error (MAE)  
  - Mean Squared Error (MSE)  
  - R² Score  

---

### 2. Predictive Maintenance (Classification)
- Built a **Random Forest Classifier** to predict maintenance requirements  
- Evaluated using:
  - Accuracy  
  - Classification Report (Precision, Recall, F1-score)  

---

## Features
- Predict CO₂ emissions based on vehicle and operational parameters  
- Detect maintenance needs to prevent breakdowns  
- Enable optimization of fuel consumption and logistics efficiency  

---

## Technologies Used

- Pandas, NumPy

---

## Impact
